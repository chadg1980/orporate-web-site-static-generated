Free New Service:
Download Patient Glucose Logs

Starting in September 2015, diabetes educators and patients will be able to use the HealthSlate Program to electronically collect, store, track and trend patients’ glucose log data as part of a diabetes education program. Educators will be able to upload data via an Android app that runs on the HealthSlate tablet. Patients will also be able to upload their glucose logs directly via the consumer HealthSlate Apps for Android and iOS. The data is securely stored and immediately available on the web-based HealthSlate Portal for use in diabetes education sessions.

This is a free service available to accredited/recognized diabetes education programs in the U.S. We intend to make the service available in certain non-U.S. regions starting in 2016. All HealthSlate tablets distributed in 2014 and 2015 will work with this new service. Additional HealthSlate tablets are available for purchase for $99 from the HealthSlate website.

At launch, this new service is expected to support all current and recent glucose meters from OneTouch®, Bayer® and Nova Max®. Starting in 2016 additional meter brands are expected to be supported. Educators intending to use the new service can join these meter vendors' free samples programs here.
