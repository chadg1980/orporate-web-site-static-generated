HealthSlate Diabetes Prevention Platform

Insurance Coverage for Diabetes Prevention Programs (DPP) is Imminent

  • Medicare coverage begins Jan 1, 2018
  • Many commercial health plans adding coverage before 2018

But Delivering DPP is Challenging

  • Traditional in-person programs have high operating costs
  • Half of consumers demand a digital/virtual program
  • Complex reporting to the CDC and payers

Tempting to Outsource to Third Party vendors BUT:

  • Loss of control over patient relationship
  • Third party programs preclude integrated care delivery/PCMH

The HealthSlate DPP Platform Addresses These Challenges

  • Enables diabetes education programs and community based organizations to implement in-person and/or digital DPP
  • Option for HealthSlate to deliver under diabetes education program or community based organization brand
  • Digital tools to greatly lower operating costs of in-person program
  • Health system retains control and care remains integrated
  • Includes access to multiple commercial health plans covering DPP

Platform Components

  • Mobile apps and website for patients
  • No transcribing of data by coaches; participants log via app
  • Web portal for program coordinator and lifestyle coaches
  • Enable digital based make-up sessions
  • Automated reporting to payers and CDC

Proven Outcomes

  • Participants engage in > 300 minutes of physical activity per week
  • Lose 5% of weight by week 26.

Reimbursement

We have contracts in place to deliver DPP on behalf of many major health plans, and can help you gain access to those plans and additional carriers in your area. We also offer a digital DSME program that has delivered outcomes equal to in-person DSME in multiple deployments.

About HealthSlate

Our mission is to help people with chronic diseases maintain their health through highly individualized programs of education, behavior modification and clinical support.
