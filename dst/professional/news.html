About HealthSlate

Our mission is to help people with chronic diseases maintain their health through highly individualized programs of education, behavior modification and clinical support.

Combining expertise in consumer behavior, mobile technology and evidenced-based medicine HealthSlate improves patients' quality of life and health outcomes.
